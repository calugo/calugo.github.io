I'm a theoretical physicist working on mathematical and computational models of evolution.

I got my degree from  [U.N.A.M.](http://www.unam.mx/) (México) and my PhD from the [University of Manchester](http://www.manchester.ac.uk/) under the supervision of  prof. [Alan J. McKane.](http://theory.physics.manchester.ac.uk/~cssp/)

Since then, I have held research posts at:

* [Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya](http://www.upc.edu/) (Barcelona, Spain), studying Calcium cycling's role in cardiac dynamics within the group of [Prof. Blas Echeberria](http://www-fa.upc.es/websfa/eupb/NOLIN/CARDIAC/Welcome.html)

* [Centro de Astrobiología, CSIC-INTA](http://www.cab.inta.es/es/inicio) (Madrid, Spain), studying properties of genotype-phenotype mappinngs and neutral evolution for the case of RNA, as part of the group of [Prof. Susanna Manrubia](http://auditore.cab.inta-csic.es/manrubia/)

Since 2014 I'm based at [The Sainsbury Laboratory](http://www.tsl.ac.uk/about.htm), studying evolution and co-evoultion of host-pathogen systems in plants, within the Bioinformatics team lead by [Dr. Dan Maclean](http://www.danmaclean.info/index.php).
